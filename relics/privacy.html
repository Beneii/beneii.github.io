Privacy Policy

Relics — Last updated 25 June 2026

Relics ("the app", "we", "us") is a daily creative-prompt app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We collect as little as possible.

Information we collect

  • Account information. Your email address, a username/display name, and an encrypted password. We use these to create and secure your account.
  • Content you create. The "relics" (images and any text) you make in response to daily prompts, along with their dates and a visibility setting you choose.
  • Usage data tied to your account. Streaks, counts, and prompt feedback (e.g. whether you found a prompt inspiring) used to power your stats.
  • Camera & photo library. Accessed only when you choose to add a photo of your creation. Images are uploaded only when you submit a relic.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, or advertising identifiers, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

How we use it

To operate your account, store and display the relics you create, calculate your stats, and send account-related emails (such as confirming your address or resetting your password). We do not sell your personal information.

Where it is stored

Account data and content are stored with our backend provider, Supabase, which hosts our database, authentication, and file storage. Transactional email is delivered through our email provider. These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy terms.

Sharing

Relics you mark as public may be visible to other signed-in users within the app. Beyond that, we share personal data only with the service providers needed to run the app, or when required by law. We never share your email address with other users.

Your choices and rights

  • Access & update. You can edit your profile in the app.
  • Change your password. Settings → Account → Change password.
  • Delete your account. Settings → Account → Delete account permanently removes your account, your relics, and your stats. This is immediate and irreversible.
  • Requests. You may also contact us to access or delete your data.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, the associated records are deleted from our database; backups, if any, are purged on a rolling basis.

Children

Relics is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be communicated in the app.
