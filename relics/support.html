Common questions

I forgot my password. On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and we'll email you a reset link.

How do I change my password? Settings → Account → Change password.

How do I delete my account? Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and everything in it.

How do I report content? Use the report option on a relic, or email us; we review reports and remove content or accounts that break our Terms of Use.
